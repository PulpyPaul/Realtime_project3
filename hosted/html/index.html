Socket Shot

Compete against your friends and show off your skills in this physics based canvas game!
  • Objective
    The objective of the game is to flick your colored balls into the bucket. Each ball that lands inside of the bucket will reward the player points!
  • Controls
    Click and drag the balls of your color to flick them. Moving past the boundry line will cause them to disconnect from your cursor
  • Frameworks/Libraries
    Node.js, P2 physics, Materialize, Socket.io
